Welcome to Care Compass



Care Compass is dedicated to eradicating poverty by serving as a beacon of hope and information hub.
We are committed to raising awareness about essential services that empower individuals in need to achieve a basic quality of life.
Whether it's finding a warm meal at a local soup kitchen, a safe haven in a homeless shelter, or access to affordable clothing at thrift stores,
Care Compass connects people with vital resources that make a tangible difference.

Care Compass Goals

Provide Essential Information

To distribute information about crucial
services such as soup kitchens, homeless
shelters, thrift stores, mobile water services,
mobile clinics, care centers,
and buy-back centers.

Facilitate
Connections

To connect sponsors, volunteers, and
donors with essential service providers
and locations, ensuring accessibility
regardless of financial constraints.

Form
Partnerships

To establish strong partnerships with
orphanages, food companies, clothing
companies, and clinics to ensure a steady
supply of resources and support.
